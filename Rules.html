KNIFE PARTY - How to play

Knife Party is a social game for you and your friends, designed to be played during the course of several hours or days.

1 - Create a game room or join one by entering its PIN and enter your nickname.

2 - You will then be given a target's name, an object (murder weapon) and the place where the murder must occur.

- The moment you get your target to voluntarily hold that specific object in the designated place, your target is considered dead.

4 - You will then be assigned your next target.

5 - If you get murdered by another player, you will be out of the game.

6 - You can use all means necessary, except force, to convince your target hold the object. Feel free to make alliances with other players.

7 - The last player standing (or the one with the highest number of kills) wins the game.
